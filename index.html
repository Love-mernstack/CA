Your CA Journey Roadmap

From your first step to becoming a Chartered Accountant, here's the complete journey outlined for you.

1

CA Foundation

Register after 12th. Appear for the Foundation exam to begin your journey.

Eligibility: Complete Class 12 from any stream and register with ICAI.
2

CA Intermediate

Clear Foundation & register for Intermediate (2 groups, 8 subjects).

Tip: Prepare group-wise and appear when confident. Both groups can be cleared together or separately.
3

Articleship

Start 3-year training under a practicing CA after clearing Group 1 of Intermediate.

Pro Insight: Choose your firm wisely. This hands-on experience builds your real-world skills.
4

CA Final

Register for Final. Appear during last 6 months of articleship.

Resources: Focus on elective subjects, case studies, and mock tests for success.
5

Become a CA

Clear Final, complete Articleship & enroll as a Chartered Accountant!

Celebration Tip: Apply for ICAI membership & proudly use "CA" before your name!
